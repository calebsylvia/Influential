Stereo

Released in 2018

Stereo was Omar's debut album that has many underrated hits such as "Ignorin" and "Amor Malo". This is what broke him into the industry putting out official music on bigger platforms.

Friends

Released in 2019

Friends is a very small project from Omar but not one to be overlooked. This album is full of some amazing songs such as "Kickback" and "Trouble".

Apolonio

Released in 2020

Apolonio is the beginning of Omar's exposure. This one featured popular artist, Kali Uchis with their song "Hey Boy". This started to take off his career and make his name known.

Ivory

Released in 2022

This album is the album that blew up and gained Omar so much popularity with his hit song "Evergreen". This song became very popular on Tik Tok, which is the platform that helped his success.

Ivory (Marfil)

Released in 2022

This is the deluxe version of the original Ivory but with some added songs and a new version of "Endlessly" which is an absolute banger of a song.

Live for Me

Released in 2023

Live for Me was a small EP that he had dropped but the songs that are on there sound angelic and beautiful, something worth checking out.
